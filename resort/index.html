A Home Away From Home

Nestled amidst lush greenery and located just 1 minute from Mandwa Jetty, The Dillzan Resort offers a perfect blend of comfort, hospitality, delicious food, and entertainment. Whether you're planning a family holiday, reunion, senior citizens' getaway, or a romantic escape, The Dillzan Resort promises an unforgettable experience.

"For our guests, there is no 'No'. We want everyone to experience warmth, comfort and freedom, just like they would in their own home."

— Dillzan Wadia, Founder & Owner

The Man Behind the Magic

From the Silver Screen
to Your Dream Escape

"
For my guests, there is no 'No'. My aim is to see people enjoy themselves and feel like they are in their own home — with warmth, comfort, and freedom.

Actor Dillzan Wadia — known for Sarfaroshi, Four Pillars of Basement, Bollywood Villa and several television shows — has channelled his passion for people into creating one of Alibaug's warmest boutique retreats. From his humble beginnings in Vadodara, running a fast-food joint at King Circle while pursuing his acting dreams at Khalsa Junior College, to starring in acclaimed films and now building resorts in Goa, Lonavala and Alibaug, Dillzan's journey is one of heart.

"This quality of hosting comes from my parents," he shares. "At our home, every guest was treated with love and humility — and that's the spirit I bring to The Dillzan Resort."

Dillzan Wadia
Actor · Founder & Owner, The Dillzan Resort
